Register Your RTX 4080 as a Worker

To register your GPU as a transcoding worker, update your agent's config.yaml:

worker:
  enabled: true
  worker_id: "DESKTOP-MAIN-4080"
  priority: 1
  max_concurrent_jobs: 2
  nvenc: true
  hevc: true
  av1: true

Workers with lower priority numbers are preferred. Your RTX 4080 should be priority 1.
